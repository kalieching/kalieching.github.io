about

Hi! I'm Kalie Ching (she/her), a research engineer at the Robotics and AI Institute (RAI) working on conditioning for robot policy learning. I previously built 3D perception systems for robots that learn skills from human videos.

I graduated from UC Berkeley with a major in computer science and a minor in data science. I taught EECS 16B as a Lab TA for four semesters, and have interned at Cruise (now GM), Microsoft Azure, and NASA's Astrobee Facility Project.

Outside of work: tennis, winter sports, gym, and character & fashion design.
